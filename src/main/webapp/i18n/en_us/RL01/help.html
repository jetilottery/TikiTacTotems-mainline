TIKI-TAC-TOE

The sacred ancestors of Polynesia are offering up exotic riches in Tiki-Tac-Toe! Win a prize when you line up three matching symbols in any row, column or diagonal. Watch for the special Tiki Sun symbol: when it appears it not only substitutes for any symbol, increasing the chances of even more wins, it also activates three Bonus turns!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Select the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Select the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Select BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Select the PLAY button to start the game. Reveal 3 matching symbols in any row, column or diagonal to win.

The Tiki Sun symbol substitutes for any symbol and awards 3 bonus turns for more chances to win!.

The Tiki Sun symbol can appear on any grid position on the first turn only.

Bonus turns cannot be re-triggered.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
